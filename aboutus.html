About Us

At Pet Toys Store, we believe that every pet deserves love, joy, and a little bit of fun! Our mission is to provide high-quality, safe, and engaging toys for pets while also making a difference in the lives of stray animals.
Our Mission: We're not just a pet toy store - we're a movement for kindness. That's why we created FurLuv Foundation, a cause dedicated to helping stray cats and dogs find food, shelter, and medical care. With every purchase, 30% of the profits goes directly to our foundation, supporting animals in need.
